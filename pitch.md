Problem Statement: Why is the old menu-based portal inefficient?

-The current student portal uses a menu-driven design that makes it difficult for users to quickly locate information. Students are required to navigate through several layers just to access basic details such as schedules, grades, or announcements. This not only consumes time but also creates confusion, especially for new users. Moreover, the system lacks adaptability, offering the same layout and experience to all users without considering individual preferences.

Industry 5.0 Justification: How will your AI empower humans rather than replace them?

-The ASSCAT AI Copilot reflects the goals of Industry 5.0 by supporting and enhancing human capabilities instead of replacing them. It functions as an intelligent assistant that provides real-time help, useful suggestions, and easier navigation for students. By automating repetitive tasks like answering common inquiries, the AI allows users to focus on more meaningful activities. At the same time, staff remain actively involved in managing and improving the system, ensuring a balanced collaboration between humans and technology.

Agile Requirements: Write three (3) User Stories using the format: "As a [User], I want [AI Feature], so that [Benefit]."

-As a Student, I want an AI-based assistant in the portal, so that I can access information faster without going through multiple menus.
-As a Student, I want the AI to support both English and Bisaya, so that I can communicate using the language I am most comfortable with.
-As a Student, I want the AI to give customized responses, so that I receive information that matches my personal needs and profile.
